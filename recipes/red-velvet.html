Red Velvet Cookies

Delectable Red Velvet Cookies

These red velvet cookies combine the classic flavors of red velvet cake with the chewy goodness of cookies. They are perfect for any occasion, with their vibrant red color and rich, chocolatey taste, finished with a hint of cream cheese flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powde
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon red food coloring
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, cocoa, and baking soda.
  3. Beat butter and sugar; add egg, vanilla, and food coloring.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake 10-12 minutes. Cool and enjoy!
